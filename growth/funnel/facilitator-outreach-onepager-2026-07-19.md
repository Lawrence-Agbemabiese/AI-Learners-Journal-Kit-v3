## VERSION 1 — OMCP centers, universities, public/nonprofit programs

### Your learners finish training. What do they leave holding?

Trainers are expected to produce employable, portfolio-ready graduates — but most programs give you no reflection or portfolio system. Building worksheets, rubrics, and templates from scratch is nobody's spare-time job.

**The Facilitator Cohort Pack is that system, ready on day one:**

- Printable student materials for every learner: the 12-week Student Portfolio Pack and the 7-day starter journal — built for low-device settings; paper works.
- A step-by-step facilitator guide: agenda, sample exercise, troubleshooting script, safety guidance.
- A timed **2-hour workshop flow** (0–120 min) plus an assessment rubric — run a portfolio clinic this month, not next term.
- The Journal Kit app for learners with computers: local, searchable, offline journal in files they keep forever (Python 3.9+; AI optional with their own key).

*The software itself is free for noncommercial educational use — this license is priced for the ready-made cohort materials, the workshop system, and facilitator support.*

**Pricing — ARM A block:**

- Per-Cohort License: **GHS 950** — one named cohort, up to 30 learners, one run (+GHS 20/learner beyond 30).
- Per-Institution Annual: **GHS 4,500** — one center or campus, unlimited cohorts, 12 months.

**Pricing — ARM B block:**

- Per-Cohort License: **GHS 950** — one named cohort, up to 30 learners, one run (+GHS 20/learner beyond 30).
- Per-Institution Annual: **GHS 4,500** — one center or campus, unlimited cohorts, 12 months.
- **Pilot: 50% off your first cohort — GHS 475.** Run one full cohort before committing further.

**After the cohort:** your learners can buy personal Portfolio Packs (GHS 49) to keep the habit going on their own.
