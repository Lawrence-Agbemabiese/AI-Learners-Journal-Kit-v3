## FAQ

### Is this official One Million Coders material?
No. It is an independent support resource designed for learners participating in coding, AI, and digital-skills programs. It complements your program and certificate; it doesn't replace either.

### I'll get a certificate. Why do I need this?
Keep the certificate — it matters. But a certificate can't show an employer *how* you work: the bugs you fixed, the prompts you refined, the project decisions you can explain. The journal builds that evidence week by week, so at interview time you're reading your own record, not straining to remember.

### Do I need to know Python already?
No coding knowledge is needed to use the journal. The PDFs work on paper or phone. The app needs Python 3.9 or newer installed (free from python.org) — the included START HERE launcher opens a friendly browser page, no commands to type.

### Does the AI part cost money? Is it required?
AI is optional. The journal and search work fully offline with no account. If you want AI answers, you bring your own API key — there are free-tier options (e.g., Groq). We never guarantee that AI answers are accurate; the journal exists so *your* understanding is the record.

### Can I use it on my phone?
Yes. The PDFs and text templates are phone-friendly, and the system also works in Google Docs, Notion, or printed form. The app itself runs on a computer with Python 3.9+.

### What if my program pauses or a portal goes down?
Your journal lives on your own machine as plain Markdown files. If classes pause, your journal doesn't.

### What's your refund policy?
Refunds are available within 7 days if you cannot access the download, the release package is incomplete, or the documented installer fails on a supported platform and we cannot provide a workaround. Because this is a digital download, refunds are not offered for change-of-mind purchases or third-party AI API behavior.
