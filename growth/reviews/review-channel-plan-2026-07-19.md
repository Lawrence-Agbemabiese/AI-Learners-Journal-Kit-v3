## Refutations

### Citations spot-checked (5 fetched)

1. **BROKEN — TikTok "~81% monthly use, #2 platform" ([Pulse Ghana], cited in §1.4 and the §2 E1 row).** I fetched the Pulse Ghana article. It covers the 26 June 2025 Sam George × TikTok meeting — child safety, creator pay, the STEM feed, and the minister's recommendation to integrate it with OMCP. It contains **no TikTok usage statistic of any kind**. The 81% figure is unsupported by every source the plan cites for it (the fetched DataReportal article text also does not carry it). The E1 channel ranking's entire audience-presence case rests on this number. What the Pulse article DOES support — the Ministry courting TikTok's STEM feed for OMCP — is real and is the better argument; use it.
2. **BROKEN — "Phase 2 cohort registration ran 15–20 July 2026, first-come, first-served ([TechFocus24]; [GhCampus])" (§1.2).** I fetched both. TechFocus24 (published 1 May 2026) says registration "is currently ongoing" with no dates and no FCFS language. GhCampus (published 26 April 2026) says registration "is now open" and tells prior applicants not to reapply — again no dates, no FCFS. Neither source contains "15–20 July 2026" or "first-come, first-served." The claim that "a fresh intake is onboarding **right now**" and the recurring-dated-window model behind the standing "registration week" package are built on sources that actually describe an open-ended registration that began in late April.
3. **PARTIALLY BROKEN — WhatsApp "~92–93% of internet users 16–64" ([DataReportal Digital 2026: Ghana]) (§1.4, §2 P2 row).** I fetched the cited page. Its article text has platform sections for YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Messenger, Reddit, and X — **no WhatsApp figures at all** (WhatsApp survey data lives, if anywhere, in the embedded slide deck, which the citation does not point to). "Ghana leads observed markets in WhatsApp share of digital population" is likewise not on the page. What the same fetch DID verify: 26.3M internet users and 74.6% penetration — those two numbers check out exactly.
4. **VERIFIED — gate-status claims (§0).** I extracted the text of the shipped `facilitator-cohort-license-guide.pdf` from today's 07:29 Facilitator ZIP: it still prints "Starter cohort: GHS 300 / Growth: GHS 750 / Institution: GHS 1,500 / Large program: GHS 3,000." I fetched the live agenticppa.com/ghana-ai-coding-journal page: it still displays the same four-tier table AND still says "Full AI Learner's Journal Kit v3.0.1 release bundle." Both of the plan's §0 gate findings are true, and the plan correctly refuses to start facilitator outreach until they are fixed.
5. **VERIFIED — Pulse/Ecofin STEM-feed courtship (June 2025 meeting)** is supported by the fetched Pulse article (minister recommended integrating TikTok's STEM feed with OMCP). Unfetched but non-load-bearing: GCTU 2,000-slot announcement, Ecofin 130-centers piece (the 130-centers figure is independently carried in the approved demand brief).

2. **Fix the TikTok evidence (§1.4, §2 E1 row).** Drop the ~81% figure or re-cite it to a source that states it (a specific DataReportal slide, GWI, or a Statista page with the number visible). Rebuild the E1 case on what your fetched sources do support: the Ministry's active STEM-feed courtship makes OMCP content algorithm-topical. If no usage number survives sourcing, say so and let E1 stand as a pure experiment.
3. **Fix the WhatsApp citation (§1.4, §2 P2 row).** Point to the specific artifact containing the 92–93% figure (slide number or alternate source), or soften to what the fetched page verifies: 26.3M internet users, 74.6% penetration, WhatsApp widely reported as Ghana's most-used platform. P2's ranking survives the softer claim.
4. **Reconcile Experiment 2's two-arm quote split with public GHS 950 list pricing (§3).** Options to put to Lawrence: (a) drop the A3 arm and vary only the pilot concession; (b) run A3 only with prospects reached before they see the site (fragile — say so); (c) accept a contaminated A3 reading and record it as such. ## Three weakest sentences, with rewrites

1. > "**Reach moment #1 — registration windows.** Phase 2 cohort registration ran **15–20 July 2026, first-come, first-served** … — i.e., a fresh intake is onboarding **right now**, and windows recur per cohort."
   Rewrite: "**Reach moment #1 — registration pushes.** Phase 2 registration opened in late April 2026 and cited coverage describes it as ongoing with no published close date (GhCampus, 26 Apr 2026; TechFocus24, 1 May 2026); treat registration attention as episodic Ministry announcements to react to, not scheduled windows to plan around."
2. > "TikTok is #2 at ~81% monthly use, and the Ministry itself courted a **TikTok STEM-feed partnership specifically to publicize OMCP**."
   Rewrite: "The Ministry itself courted TikTok's STEM feed specifically to publicize OMCP (June 2025 meeting, verified) — OMCP content is institutionally pushed onto the platform; no reliable Ghana TikTok usage rate could be verified, so E1 is sized as an experiment, not a reach assumption."
3. > "Budget the 90 days for ~1,000–2,500 engineered visits: enough for Experiments 3 and 1, not for the ceiling scenarios."
   Rewrite: "Budget the 90 days for ~1,000–2,500 engineered visits — assuming X WhatsApp sends/forwards at Y% click-through and Z TikTok views at W% link-tap (all assumptions, logged for the retro): enough for Experiments 3 and 1 if the assumptions hold, and the Day-45 E1 review is the checkpoint if they don't."
