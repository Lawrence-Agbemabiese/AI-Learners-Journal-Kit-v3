# Ghana Launch Assets

This folder contains the Day 1-15 launch copy and packaging source for the Ghana-first AI Learner's Journal Kit campaign.

Primary funnel:

1. Free 7-day AI coding journal
2. Paid Student Portfolio Pack
3. Facilitator/cohort license inquiries

Primary audience:

- high-school students
- university students
- coding club members
- bootcamp participants
- learners in national coding and digital-skills programs

Recommended public positioning:

Designed to support learners participating in coding, AI, and digital-skills programs such as Ghana's One Million Coders. Independent resource. Not an official government product.
