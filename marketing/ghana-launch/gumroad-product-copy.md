## Long description

You can finish a coding course and still struggle to explain what you actually learned.

The AI Learner's Journal Kit fixes that.

It gives you a simple weekly system for recording what you studied, what you built, what went wrong, how you used AI tools, and how to turn each project into a short portfolio story.

Designed for students in Ghana and across Africa, this pack works especially well for learners joining national coding, AI, data, cybersecurity, and digital-skills programs.
