# Refund Policy

This file provides a storefront-ready refund policy template for the paid digital product.

## Suggested Policy

Because this is a downloadable digital product, refunds should be limited to clear product access or functionality problems.

Recommended refund window: 7 days from purchase.

Refunds are appropriate when:

- The customer cannot access the purchased download.
- The release ZIP is corrupted or missing required files.
- The documented installer fails on a supported platform and support cannot provide a workaround.
- The product page materially misrepresented the current feature set.

Refunds are not normally appropriate when:

- The customer changes their mind after downloading.
- The customer expected custom consulting, tutoring, or setup beyond the documented product.
- The customer does not have Python installed and declines to install it.
- A third-party AI API is unavailable, rate-limited, changes behavior, or produces an unsatisfactory answer.
- The customer uses an unsupported operating system.

## Storefront Text

Use this concise version on the sales page:

> Refunds are available within 7 days if you cannot access the download, the release package is incomplete, or the documented installer fails on a supported platform and we cannot provide a workaround. Because this is a digital download, refunds are not offered for change-of-mind purchases or third-party AI API behavior.

## Support Before Refund

Ask customers to contact support before requesting a refund when the issue is installation-related. Most setup issues can be resolved by confirming Python installation, PATH setup, and the correct release ZIP.
